What is Teamfight Tactics?

TFT is a complex auto battler where you compete against seven other players to be the last one standing. Success hinges on four key areas: Champions, Items, Economy, and Positioning.

  • Drafting & Combos: You recruit champions from a shared pool, using Gold earned from rounds to purchase units, level up, and reroll your shop. Combining champions with matching Traits (Synergies) grants powerful bonuses to your team.
  • Items: Collect components from monster (PvE) rounds or the "Carousel" shared draft. Combining two components activates a new, powerful ability or stat boost, which is key to unlocking your team's true potential.
  • The Carousel: After every third round of a stage, players select a free champion with a random item from a rotating pool. The selection order is strategically determined by player health, with the lowest health choosing first.

Adapt as you go—there are unlimited winning strategies, making every match a unique challenge.

The Meta Game: Economy and Phases

Winning in TFT isn't just about the final board; it's about managing your "Economy" through three distinct phases: the early game, the mid game, and the late game. A strong economy allows for flexible decision-making and power spikes.

  • Gold Management: Gold is earned through base income, winning/losing streaks, and interest (up to 5 Gold per 50 Gold held). Knowing when to "roll down" (spend gold) or "fast 8" (level up quickly) defines your strategy.
  • Phases of Play: The "Early Game" (Stages 1-3) focuses on finding stable, powerful openers. The "Mid Game" (Stages 3-5) involves leveling up and finalizing your core composition. The "Late Game" (Stage 5+) is dominated by finding 5-cost units and optimal itemization for your main "Carries".

The Philosophy: Adaptation is Key

Unlike traditional strategy games where you pre-select a path, TFT is a game of constant adaptation and flexibility. Every single match presents unique variables: the items you receive, the champions that appear in your shop, and the Augments (or Power Ups) you are offered.

  • Unpredictable Strategy: There are no "cookie-cutter" builds. A top-tier Tactician must pivot (change strategy) quickly when faced with unfavorable drafts or strong opponents.
  • Mastering the Economy: Gold management is a critical skill. Knowing when to utilize your win or loss streak for bonus gold, and when to spend your savings for a crucial power spike, dictates your success.
  • The Power of Positioning: Even the strongest composition can lose if your units are poorly placed. Protect your "Carries" (damage dealers) in the backline and position your "Tanks" to absorb key enemy abilities.
